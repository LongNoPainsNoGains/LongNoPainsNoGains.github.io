每次写文章都要找下自己写过的文章把宏包放上去，而且总是记性不好，即学即忘得那种，不如把经常用的一些宏包放这拉倒吧~省的自掘坟墓扒了半天都找不到自己以前用过的模板。。。。。顺便记录一点有用的东西，就算是学这个留下的笔记吧。。。。。自己也不善于管理自己的笔记，虽然有印象笔记，自我整理的感觉也还算可以，可是嘛，东西一旦多了，看着就头大，谁叫自己平时没事瞎逛收藏了杂七杂八的东西呢。。。。。。怪我喽。。。。。。对，是怪我。。。。。都列出来了，反正不会报错的。
这里可以学习到一些有用的LaTeX技巧，当然，也可以到用的时候再学，不需要全部都学，只是个工具罢了，没必要搞得太清楚（说我不思进取我认了）。这里有流程图的画法，当然上面那个链接也有。话说Texmaker真的坑呀，在我这解决不了画流程图的问题，无奈之下下了Winedt这个编辑器才解决了这个问题。。。。。。

花了一个晚上的时间画的流程图，不过结果还可以，哈哈~还是挺简单的,源自于此。做表格的这里写的挺好的。

% 流程图定义基本形状
\tikzstyle{startstop} = [rectangle, rounded corners, minimum width = 2cm, minimum height=1cm,text centered, draw = black, fill = red!40]
\tikzstyle{io} = [trapezium, trapezium left angle=70, trapezium right angle=110, minimum width=2cm, minimum height=1cm, text centered, draw=black, fill = blue!40]
\tikzstyle{process} = [rectangle, minimum width=3cm, minimum height=1cm, text centered, draw=black, fill = yellow!50]
\tikzstyle{decision} = [diamond, aspect = 2, text centered, draw=black, fill = green!30]
% 箭头形式
\tikzstyle{arrow} = [->,>=stealth]

\begin{figure}[htbp]
\centering
\begin{tikzpicture}[node distance=1.5cm]
%定义流程图具体形状
\node (start) [startstop] {start};
\node (pro1) [process,  xshift=2cm, right of =start, align=center ] {Choose the \\ initial parameter};                  % left of=start
\node (pro2) [process, below of =pro1, yshift=-0.2cm,align=center] {Generate random \\ initial population};
\node (pro3) [process, left of=pro2, xshift= -2cm,align=center] {Calculate the \\ cost of each \\ raindrop};
\node (pro4) [process, below of=pro3, yshift= -0.2cm,align=center] {Determine the\\ intensity of flow};
\node (pro5) [process, right of=pro4, xshift= 3cm,align=center] {Flow streams\\ to the rivers};
\node (pro6) [process, right of=pro5, xshift= 2cm,align=center] {Folw river \\ to the sea};
\node (dec1) [decision, above of=pro6, yshift= 1cm,align=center] {Have a  lower\\ function  value than\\ corresponding river?};
\node (dec2) [decision, right of=dec1, xshift= 5cm,align=center] {Have a lower\\ function value than\\ sea?};
%\node (pro7) [process, right of=dec1, xshift= 3cm] {PROCESS 7};
\node (pro8) [process, above of=dec1, yshift= 2cm,align=center] {Exchange the position\\ of the stream with \\ the corresponding river };
\node (pro7) [process, below of=dec2, yshift= -2cm,align=center] {Exchange the position \\ of the river \\ with the sea};
\node (dec3) [decision, below of=pro7, yshift= -1cm,align=center] {Is the evaporation\\ condition satisfied?};
\node (pro9) [process, left of=dec3, xshift= -3.5cm,align=center] {Create clouds \\ and then  start\\ raining process};
\node (pro10) [process, left of=pro9, xshift= -2cm,align=center] {Decrease the value \\ of the $d_{max}$};
\node (dec4) [decision, left of=pro10, xshift= -3cm,align=center] {Is the  convergence\\ criteria satisfied?};
\node (end) [startstop, below of=dec4,yshift= -3cm] {end};
